ArbOS is the Layer 2 EVM hypervisor that facilitates the execution environment of L2 Arbitrum.
ArbOS accounts for and manages network resources, produces blocks from incoming messages, and
operates its instrumented instance of geth for smart contract execution.
For more information please see the source code and documentation.
